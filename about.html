A little about me,



My name is Goran Katinic, I’m a 34 year old single dad. I was born in Slavonski Brod, Croatia on November the 13th, 1987 just before the start of the Yugoslav War. In the early 90’s my mother & I moved to small town in Germany about an hour east of Munich. We lived in Germany for 6 years before moving to Atlanta, Georgia in June of 1998. After just a short 5 months before we moved to Salt Lake City, Utah in late December of 1998. I’m bilingual in 4 languages (Bosnian, Croatian, English, Serbian, & understand a little German still.

I received my UX/UI education from the University of Utah in 2022. Before my UX/UI Career I was a Certified HVAC Technician/Installer. I have a degree in Heating, Ventilation, & Air Conditioning from Fortis College. I also have a Flagger Certificate, Rocky Mountain Gas Certification, Trac-Pipe Certificate, Universal Refrigeration Certification, & Heat Pump Certificate. I have worked on Residential, Commercial, & Industrial Projects. Which led me doing Automated Building Controls at The University of Utah Health & Science Campus. After Injuring my lower back and then re, re-injuring it over time I have decided to give up on my HVAC career and pursue a new career in design.

Some of my hobbies include watching sports, fishing, boating, camping, anything outdoors in the summer the kiddo & I enjoy. We like to hibernate during winter while learning new materials & being creative with new projects. I love to do kayak fishing, I’m a member of the Kayak Bass Fishing(KBF) group & the Kayak Fishing Utah(KFU) group.

Feel free to look around and I hope I can be of service!
