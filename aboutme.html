Hey Welcome!

My name is Goran Katinic & I’m a 34 year old UX/UI Designer out of Salt Lake City, Utah. I was born in Slavonski Brod, Croatia in 1987. In the early 90’s my family & I moved for 6 years to a small town in Germany, about an hour east of Munich. In 1998 we moved to Atlanta, Georgia for 5 months before we made it to Salt Lake City, Utah in the end of 1998. I’m bilingual in 4 languages (Bosnian, Croatian, English, Serbian, & understand a little German.


When im not at the computer working and learning I like to spend my time outdoors. I love spending my time at the lake kayak fishing with my beautiful daughter.


I received my UX/UI education from the University of Utah in 2022. Before my UX/UI Career I was a Certified HVAC Technician/Installer. I have a degree in Heating, Ventilation, & Air Conditioning from Fortis College. I also have a Flagger Certificate, Rocky Mountain Gas Certification, Trac-Pipe Certificate, Universal Refrigeration Certification, & Heat Pump Certificate. Worked on Residential, Commercial, & Industrial Projects. Which led me doing Controls at The University of Utah Health & Science Campus.
